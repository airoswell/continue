How it works

Search and discover
Just search for stuffs that you are interested in. Discover posts for sale or free stuffs, reviews of products, check out the timeline of each items that are publicly viewable set by their owners.

Discover sharable stuffs in your neighborhood. You might some day need a barbecue set and on Continue you discover that your friendly neighbor has one ready for share.

 Document your item
Add a record of your stuffs in the system, either for sale, sharing with friends and neighbors, or just being self-organized.

Update the records if you like. Each item will have its individual timeline for you to browse, where you can trace back in time and remind yourself how you have interacted with it, and the people involved...


Write about it
Share with others your review and personal experiences with products you have. Combining with the timeline, people can get more sense on how well the product does, how endurable it is, as time goes by.

Needless to say, your experiences are important to the community.
 Share, transfer your item
The age of Internet is the age of sharing.

Items that are set to be public viewable and available can be searched by other users. We encourage to share or (even more generously) give your idle or no-longer-needed items to people in need. But of course you are also welcome to post items for sale too.

Send requests to the owners when you see available items you need in public posts by the owners. We encourage you to also keep track of item transfer using the transfer button .
