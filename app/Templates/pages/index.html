How WorldSheet works

 Timeline for anything
Build Timelines for your cars, pets, products, anything. Keep records of how they change, grow and perform, moments you enjoy with them, or anything you want to keep track of.


 Write about it
Write posts to share, sell, review, or just jot down anything about your belongings, pets, plants or products.
 Search and discover
Just search for stuffs that you are interested in. Discover posts of sale, freebies or reviews of products, check out the timeline of items that are publicly viewable set by their generous owners.

With a click of button, follow areas to view new posts and updates from those areas, right in your dashboard. Stay tune with your neighboring community!
 Share, transfer, enjoy.
The age of Internet is the age of sharing.

Items that are set to be public viewable and available can be searched by other users. We encourage to share or (even more generously) give your idle or no-longer-needed items to people in need.

But of course you are also welcome to post items for sale too.

Send requests to the owners when you see available items you need in public posts by the owners.

We encourage the owners to also keep a record of the item-transfer, using the transfer button . Soon you will be able to follow the footstep of your item on its timeline, as it adventures through the human world.
